Frequently Asked Questions

What is GreenSpark?

GreenSpark is a gamified environmental education platform that empowers individuals and communities to take eco-friendly actions and earn rewards for their positive impact on the planet.

How do I earn points?

You earn points by completing eco-challenges, participating in quizzes, and contributing to community projects. Points can be redeemed for various rewards and benefits.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, GreenSpark is completely free for individual users. We partner with educational institutions and corporate sponsors to support the platform.

Our Future Vision

We're just getting started. Our roadmap includes launching a dedicated mobile application, partnering with corporate sponsors for bigger rewards, and integrating with educational institutions to make GreenSpark a core part of their curriculum.
