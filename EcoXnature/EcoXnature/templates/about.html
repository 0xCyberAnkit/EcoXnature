Frequently Asked Questions

What is EcoXnature?

EcoXnature is a gamified environmental education platform that empowers individuals and communities to take eco-friendly actions and earn rewards for their positive impact on the planet.

How do I earn points?

You earn points by completing eco-challenges, participating in quizzes, and contributing to community projects. Points can be redeemed for various rewards and benefits.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, EcoXnature is completely free for individual users. We partner with educational institutions and corporate sponsors to support the platform.
